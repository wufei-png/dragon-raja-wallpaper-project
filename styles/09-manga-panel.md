# 中文名：黑白漫画分镜

## 视觉目标

高对比黑白、速度线、强阴影、叙事瞬间。

## Prompt 片段

```text
black and white manga panel, high contrast ink, dramatic shadows,
speed lines, cinematic panel composition, intense close-up and wide negative space,
rough ink texture, no color
```

## 负面词补充

```text
no gray muddy rendering, no full-color anime, no text bubbles unless requested
```

## 构图建议

适合做单张强分镜。壁纸版建议无文字，无对话框。
