# style_id: ink-mythic
# 中文名：水墨神话风

## 视觉目标

墨色、水势、云气、龙影、留白。适合东方水域与古城意象。

## Prompt 片段

```text
Chinese ink wash mythic fantasy, flowing ink, mist, water currents,
ancient bronze structures, vast negative space, subtle gold accents,
dragon silhouette emerging from clouds and water, elegant and ominous
```

## 负面词补充

```text
no xianxia sword cultivator, no palace fantasy romance, no cute dragon, no festive red-gold poster
```

## 构图建议

适合作为番外风格。不要让它完全变成传统仙侠，要保留“龙族”的现代/炼金/古文明异质感。
