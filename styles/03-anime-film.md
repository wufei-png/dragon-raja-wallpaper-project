# style_id: anime-film
# 中文名：日系动画电影感

## 视觉目标

电影关键帧。背景、光线、空气感和情绪优先，人物不必占太大比例。

## Prompt 片段

```text
cinematic anime film still, wide shot, detailed atmospheric background,
soft volumetric light, subtle color grading, melancholic mood,
careful composition, expressive silhouettes, high production value
```

## 负面词补充

```text
no flat character-only portrait, no cheap TV anime look, no overexposed colors
```

## 构图建议

用远景或大全景，保留天气、地平线、城市结构、深海空间。
