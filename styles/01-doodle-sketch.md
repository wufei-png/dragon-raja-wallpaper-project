# style_id: doodle-sketch
# 中文名：简笔画 / 涂鸦表情风

## 视觉目标

像随手画出来的漫画梗图：低细节、粗黑线、白底、少量点缀色、表情直接。

## Prompt 片段

```text
rough hand-drawn doodle, thick messy black lines, white background, minimal details,
deadpan expression, simple comic composition, sparse color accents, intentionally rough sketch,
flat white space, humorous contrast
```

## 负面词补充

```text
no realistic rendering, no complex background, no highly detailed painting, no glossy 3D,
no polished anime key visual
```

## 构图建议

主体大，背景少。允许加一个手写牌子或对话框，但最终壁纸版建议无文字。
