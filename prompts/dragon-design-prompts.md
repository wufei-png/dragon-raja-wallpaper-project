# 龙形象提示词库

## 非普通西幻龙

```text
not a generic western dragon, not a cute dragon, not a pet, not a mount,
an ancient dragon king like a divine catastrophe, part god and part prehistoric organism,
with alchemical markings, bronze-like bones, immense scale, intelligent golden eyes
```

## 深海龙

```text
a colossal deep-sea dragon silhouette, half-bone half-living, moving like a black ocean current,
ancient scales covered with pale bioluminescent alchemical patterns,
only one enormous golden eye visible in the darkness
```

## 青铜龙 / 炼金龙

```text
an ancient bronze-and-fire dragon king, scales fused with oxidized bronze armor,
molten light glowing between plates, alchemical dragon script across the body,
a godlike mechanical-organic presence
```

## 白王

```text
a White King presence rising like a cold divine catastrophe, not necessarily a full dragon body,
white silk, cocoon, sacred remains, pale scales or skeletal wings, crimson Red Well mist around it,
tragic and ritualistic, not cute, not heroic, not decorative
```

## 地铁尼伯龙根巨龙

```text
a mountain-sized dragon integrated into the underground tunnel walls,
one vast golden eye opening at the end of the subway tracks,
its body implied by stone, metal, dust, and shadow
```

## 龙侍参孙 / 三峡水战

```text
a massive dragon-servant like Samson, black armored spine breaking through the Yangtze storm,
hooked teeth, cold golden eye, body half-hidden by rain, whirlpools and underwater explosions,
not a generic sea monster, not a western dragon
```

## 黑天鹅港古龙骨

```text
an ancient dragon skeleton on the deck of a Soviet icebreaker, pale bone wings,
one faint golden eye beginning to wake, half corpse and half mythic disaster,
not a museum fossil, not a dinosaur skeleton
```

## 奥丁 / 尼伯龙根神影

```text
Odin as a godlike Nibelungen administrator, dark-gold armor, blue cloak,
an eight-legged horse, curved golden spear Gungnir, a thin white fate line through rain,
not a normal knight, not a medieval battlefield
```
